About Secure Vision

Secure Vision is a powerful AI solution designed to detect face-swap deepfake videos, ensuring media integrity and protecting against misinformation. By leveraging advanced technologies like Video Vision Transformer (ViViT), Convolutional Block Attention Module (CBAM), and Depthwise Separable Convolution (DSC), we offer a scalable and efficient detection mechanism to safeguard digital platforms.

Key Features

Advanced AI Algorithms

ViViT combined with CBAM and DSC enhances the detection of manipulated videos with high accuracy.

Real-Time Detection

Our system processes videos in real-time, ensuring quick responses to potential threats.

Scalable Infrastructure

Our AI solution is built to handle large datasets, making it ideal for media platforms and security agencies.

Use Cases

Secure Vision is designed to protect various sectors from the harmful impact of deepfakes.

  • Journalism: Verify the authenticity of video reports and prevent misinformation.
  • Social Media: Automatically detect and flag harmful deepfake content to maintain platform trust.
  • Security Agencies: Identify manipulated videos in critical scenarios such as political campaigns or social engineering attacks.
